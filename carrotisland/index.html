Projects

We created quite a few small games in the Fall of 2013. Looking for an underwater beat-um up? Look no further than Swag Shark. Windows 8 Store. How about an asteroids defense game? In 3D!? Asteroids 3D. Windows 8 Store. What about a dynamic, text-based horror game? Try out Sonny Jim - Adventure Through Hyde Park! GitHub. Sonny Jim was featured on IndieGames.com!

Carrot Island has also produced a completely browser agnostic extension. (almost, sorry IE) We call it The Interrobanger. It automagically replaces instances of '!?', '?!', and so on with the interrobang character.
